Local-only

Nothing ever leaves your device

No account required. Your transactions and balances live entirely on your phone — private by default, for anyone who'd rather not put their finances in the cloud.

Questions

Frequently asked

Is Crystal Wallet free?

Yes. Core features — tracking, budgets, multi-account balances — are never paywalled or ad-supported.

Which platforms will it launch on?

Crystal Wallet is being built for both iOS and Android, with Android as the initial launch platform.

Do I need an account to use it?

No. You can use Crystal Wallet entirely offline in local-only mode, with no sign-up required. Creating an account is only needed if you want cloud sync across devices.

What currencies are supported?

Crystal Wallet supports multi-currency tracking, with QAR as the primary currency and full support for others.
